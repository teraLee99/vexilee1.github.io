About

Hi there!

I have always loved capturing nature through a lens. Through Vexilee, I have curated a space where I can share what I see. From photos to videos to blog posts - you can expect to find a wide variety of media.

I use the tagline 'a collection of nonsense' to express that I realize none of the things I share are profound. Not in the way that nothing matters and you'll find no semblance of sense here; moreso, that I hope to convey my desire to not take life too seriously. I like to look at things and create stuff, and share it with others. I'm not sophisticated in the slightest, just a person taking in and experiencing life.

Thank you for being here!
